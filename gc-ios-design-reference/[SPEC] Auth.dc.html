Sign in & sign up

Two separate pages — Sign in and Create account — no segmented toggle, so each stays uncrowded. Sign-up adds a name field, terms line, and a "new password" hint; sign-in adds a "Forgot?" link. Email / password with a show-hide eye, then Google and Apple. After creating an account, an optional Choose your icon step (section B) lets users pick a sprite. The Apple button follows Apple's rule — black on light, white on dark. Logo mark is a placeholder.

These are live. The footer link moves between the two pages, tap the eye to reveal the password, and type into the fields.

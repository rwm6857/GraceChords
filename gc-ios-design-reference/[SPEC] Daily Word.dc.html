Daily Word

The M'Cheyne reader is the focus — forward reading through the day's chapters, not a full Bible app. Compact chrome, maximum reading space, serif body type. Tap any verse to select (one tap), then copy via the contextual button. Translation picker spans languages incl. right-to-left; swipe or tap a chapter chip to move through the day. Lorem placeholder stands in for real passages.

NEW

Alt direction · landing-first

Instead of the Daily Word tab dropping you straight into the reader, it opens a landing hub. The hub leads with a daily devotional from The Gospel Coalition (RSS, tied to one of the day's chapters), surfaces today's M'Cheyne reading, and routes you onward. The devotional is a long read, so it gets its own page — with a backlink to TGC. The reader and the devotional each gain a back chevron to the landing (this alt only). These three phones are live — tap through them.

NEW

Offline downloads · exploring

When there's no connection, the reader makes clear which translations are usable. The translation picker shows each one's state — downloaded, downloadable (with size), or downloading — and an offline banner explains the limit. Choosing and deleting downloads is managed in Settings → Offline & downloads. The daily devotional streams from TGC, so it stays online-only. Live — tap a download icon, or cancel an in-progress one.

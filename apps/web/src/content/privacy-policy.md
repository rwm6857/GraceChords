# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 3rd, 2026
**Applies to:** the GraceChords website ([https://gracechords.com](https://gracechords.com)) and the GraceChords mobile app (collectively, the "Service").

GraceChords ("GraceChords," "we," "us," or "our") is a free, non-commercial worship tool operated by Ryan Moore, an individual based in the U.S. state of Georgia. We take your privacy seriously and collect as little information as the Service needs to work. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices and rights you have.

We are not a commercial business and do not profit from your data. We do not sell it, share it for advertising, or use it to track you.

---

## 1. Information we collect

**Account information.** When you create an account, we collect:

- Your **email address**
- Your **first and last name**

You may create an account directly (email + password) or through **Sign in with Google** or **Sign in with Apple**. When you use those options, the provider shares your name and email with us so we can create your account. If you use Sign in with Apple, you may choose Apple's "Hide My Email" feature, in which case we receive a private relay address instead of your real email. We do not receive your password from these providers.

**Content you create.** To provide the Service, we store the content you make within it — for example, your setlists, song key preferences, view settings, and reading-plan progress. This is stored so it's available to you across your devices.

**Technical information (infrastructure).** We do not run analytics or tracking. However, like any online service, our hosting and infrastructure providers automatically process limited technical data — such as your IP address and standard request logs — to deliver content, keep the Service secure, and prevent abuse. This is not used to profile or track you, and we do not build advertising or behavioral profiles from it.

**Essential storage / cookies.**

- On the **website**, we use a strictly necessary cookie to keep you signed in. It is not used for tracking or advertising.
- In the **app**, we use secure on-device storage for the same purpose.

We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party tracking cookies.

---

## 2. What we do NOT do

To be clear about our practices, we do **not**:

- Run analytics or usage tracking
- Show advertising of any kind
- Use remarketing or retargeting services
- Sell, rent, or share your personal information
- Send marketing, newsletter, or promotional emails
- Knowingly collect information from children under 13

The only email we send is an **account verification link** when you sign up or when needed to secure your account.

## 3. How we use your information

We use the limited information above only to:

- Create and maintain your account
- Authenticate you and keep you signed in
- Save and sync the content you create in the Service
- Send account-related emails (e.g., email verification)
- Keep the Service secure and operational

## 4. Legal bases for processing (GDPR / UK GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, we process your personal data on these legal bases:

- **Performance of a contract** — to create your account and provide the Service you requested.
- **Legitimate interests** — to keep the Service secure and prevent abuse, balanced against your rights.
- **Consent** — where consent is required (for example, certain uses of storage/cookies), which you may withdraw at any time.

## 7. Data retention

We keep your account information and content for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your associated personal information and content, except where we are legally required to retain limited records. Backups are purged on a routine cycle.

## 8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and reputable infrastructure providers. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Your rights

### If you are in the EEA or UK (GDPR / UK GDPR)

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restrict** or **object** to certain processing
- **Portability** — receive your data in a portable format
- **Withdraw consent** at any time, where processing is based on consent
- **Lodge a complaint** with your local data protection authority

### If you are a California resident (CCPA / CPRA)

You have the right to:

- **Know** what personal information we collect and how it's used
- **Access** and receive a copy of your personal information
- **Correct** inaccurate personal information
- **Delete** your personal information
- **Opt out of the sale or sharing** of personal information — **we do not sell or share your personal information**, so there is nothing to opt out of
- **Limit the use of sensitive personal information** — we do not collect sensitive personal information for such purposes
- **Non-discrimination** — we will never treat you differently for exercising your rights

For reference, the only category of personal information we collect under the CCPA is **identifiers** (name and email address). We collect it to provide the Service, retain it as described above, and do not disclose it for any commercial purpose.

### Exercising your rights

You can exercise any of these rights — including deleting your account and data — by emailing **ryan@gracechords.com**. We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request.

## 10. Account deletion

You may delete your account and associated data at any time:

- **In the app:** through the in-app account deletion option in Settings.
- **By email:** contact **ryan@gracechords.com** and we will delete your account.

Deleting your account removes your personal information and content from the active Service, subject to the retention exceptions in Section 7.

## 11. Children's privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us personal information, contact **ryan@gracechords.com** and we will delete it.

If you are in a jurisdiction where the minimum age for data consent is higher than 13 (for example, parts of the EEA), you must meet your local minimum age, or have parental consent, to create an account.

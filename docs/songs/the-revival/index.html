The Revival

Turn to this desolate land laid in waste
Oh God of heaven, Lord, hear our desperate cries for mercy
Forgive our sins and our iniquities
Restore and heal this land

Now from these ashes we will start to build
A new foundation from the fallen ruins of this land
Come burn the idols raised above your name
Fire of the Holy Spirit, come

With fire of revival, Lord, consume this land
Let the word of truth restore this barren land
River of your grace, Lord, let it overflow
Wind of the Holy Spirit, blow

Oh, the splendor of your glorious day
Come and fill this land
Oh, the kingdom of the Lord our God
Reign forevermore

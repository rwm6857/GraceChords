Grace on Grace

When I was weak, You carried me
Mercy like rain came covering
Line upon line and day by day
You keep pouring grace on grace

Grace on grace, never ending
You are faithful, You are near
Grace on grace, for each moment
Jesus, You are always here

When fear speaks loud, Your truth remains
You steady hearts and heal our pain
Morning to night we lift this praise
You keep pouring grace on grace

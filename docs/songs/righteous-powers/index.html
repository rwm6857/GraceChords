Righteous Powers

Your righteous power wonderfully surrounds us
Your mighty hand will hold us in your grace
and even in despair we stand unshaken
We seek the face of Jesus whom we love

Although the suffering of the past surrounds me
and though I’m walking on the road of pain
My soul, entrusted to this sure salvation,
will see the coming day of victory

As Jesus took the bitter cup of suffering,
so too we will partake with joyful hearts
And as we fill the lack of Christ’s affliction,
For Jesus and his gospel we will live

By his righteous power fight in faith for Jesus
For by our faith we hold to Christ alone
We’ll hold onto your everlasting promise
Until we see the day of glory come

Holy Spirit Come

Holy Spirit come,
Come and fill this place
Jesus the way, the truth and the life
Come lead us to him

We bow down on our knees,
And lift our holy hands
We bow before our Lord Jesus Christ
And worship at his feet

With mercy and by grace,
Lord come upon this place
Wash away the sins of our past
And purify us again

Lord Yeshua we lift you high in this place
Lord Jesus, we give you all honor and praise
We give you our hearts and all of our lives
Lord may your will be done
